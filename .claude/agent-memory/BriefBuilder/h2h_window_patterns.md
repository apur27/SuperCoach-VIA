## Brisbane Lions vs Fremantle (R13 2026)
Window: last 9 meetings (2020–2026).

**Why:** Both clubs entered competitive/finals-contention era from approximately 2019 (Brisbane) and 2022 (Fremantle). Going back to 2020 captures Brisbane's full top-4 arc without the asymmetric-quality pre-rebuild era. Perth Stadium vs Gabba venue split is meaningful in this matchup.

**How to apply:** For Brisbane Lions matchups, use 2020 as the floor year to capture their rebuild-to-premiership arc. For Fremantle, their competitive window starts ~2022; meetings pre-2022 can be included for volume but should be labelled as pre-competitive-era for Frem.
