Traeger Park in Alice Springs hosts AFL matches as a neutral venue. Key data points from matches_2026.csv and matches_2023.csv:
- 2023 R16: Melbourne vs GWS at Traeger Park (3,413 attendance), GWS won 47–45 (2-point margin)
- 2026 R13: Melbourne vs GWS at Traeger Park (6,374 attendance), GWS won 119–70 (49-point margin)

Attendance is very low (3,000–6,000 range), reflecting the remote venue. Prior Traeger Park meeting (2023) was also a GWS win.
