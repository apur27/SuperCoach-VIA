## R13 2026: Dunkley Josh (Brisbane Lions)

Dunkley was selected as Brisbane Lions #5 on disposal rank (predicted 20). His 2026 disposal mean (20.0) is materially down from 2025 (25.5) — a 5.5 disposal/game decline. However his tackle mean (5.8 season, 6.0 last 5) is elite-tier. This is the Macrae-archetype: high-impact player whose disposals understate game presence.

**Decision:** Retained as #5 by disposal rank. Tackle column flagged explicitly in the per-player profile and methodology. Did not substitute him for another player — his disposal rank qualified him; the flag is additive information for FootyStrategy.

**How to apply:** When a tracked player's 2026 mean is >4 disposals below their 2025 mean, scan their tackle column. If tackles are top-tier (>5.0 mean), add a Macrae-archetype flag in the per-player note section rather than substituting them out.
