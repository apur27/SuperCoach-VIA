BriefBuilder timestamps sometimes post-date the fixture. The entire Round-14 2026 batch (8 briefs under `docs/coaches-strategy-corner/*round-14*.md`) was generated 2026-06-08/09 for matches played 2026-06-04 to 2026-06-08. Because assembly happened after the bounce, each brief's own season-record and head-to-head tables already report the **final result** (e.g. "Adelaide def. Geelong by 1", "Includes 2026 R14 result", "PA def WC — post-prediction actual result"; the Hawthorn brief states "Pre-match brief assembled post-game").

**Why it matters:** a forward-looking, pre-bounce `<!-- FOOTYSTRATEGY INSERT -->` interpretation layer cannot be written honestly over a document that already contains the outcome — the interpretation would predict a result the same page reports. This is the manufactured-certainty / incoherence trap the council rejects at hard-rule level.

**How to apply:** before completing an interpretation layer, check `Generated by BriefBuilder ... on <date>` vs the fixture `Date:` in the header, and grep the H2H/season tables for the fixture's own result row. If the brief post-dates the match, the honest dispositions are (a) **withdraw** from the forward-looking published surface, or (b) reframe as a **retrospective / post-mortem** (prediction-vs-actual — a different document class, a full rewrite, not an interpretation fill). Do not complete it as a preview. F13 (Sprint 2, 2026-07-07) withdrew all 8 R14 briefs on this basis: banner added, `FOOTYSTRATEGY INSERT` markers neutralised (no analysis fabricated), handed to Gaffer for the delink/pull-or-reframe call. Note: the 7 Round-13 briefs share the same defect and were out of F13 scope — likely a follow-up. See [[coach_anonymity_lint]].
