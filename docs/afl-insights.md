## Round 21 — Week in Review

**Disposal leaders (rounds 1–20):** Nick Daicos (Collingwood) leads the competition at **34.8 per game** **[data]**, ahead of Bailey Smith (Geelong) at **32.4** **[data]** and Harry Sheezel (North Melbourne) at **31.7** **[data]** — well clear of the league mean of **15.03 per game** **[data]**.

**Ladder:** Fremantle have extended their lead after Round 20 to **16-2** (64 points, 142.0%) **[data]**, now **12** **[data]** competition points clear of 2nd-placed Sydney (**13-5**, 52 points) **[data]** — the largest top-two gap of the season to date.

**Watch in Round 21:** Lachie Neale (Brisbane Lions) is the model's top projected disposal getter at **30.0** **[data]**, edging Nasiah Wanganeen-Milera (St Kilda), Harry Sheezel (North Melbourne) and Nick Daicos (Collingwood), each projected at **29.0** **[data]**.

**Tactical note:** Contested possessions remain the clearest predictor of clearance output (r = **+0.75** **[data]**); Clayton Oliver (GWS) leads both — **15.61** contested possessions per game **[data]** and **7.94** clearances per game **[data]** — the dataset's cleanest evidence that stoppage dominance and midfield disposal volume are the same skill expressed twice.

*Methodology: disposal-leader figures, the league mean, and the contested-possessions/clearances correlation are read from `docs/afl-stat-leaders-2026.md` (rounds 1–20 per-game aggregates over `data/player_data/`, auto-generated by `update_team_analysis.py`). Ladder figures — W-L record, points, percentage, and the top-two points gap — are read from the Round 20 ladder in `docs/afl-finals-2026.md`, itself derived from `data/matches/matches_2026.csv`. Round 21 disposal projections are read from `data/prediction/next_round_21_prediction_20260720_2007.csv`.*
