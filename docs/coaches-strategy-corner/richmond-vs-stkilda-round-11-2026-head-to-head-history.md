## The 2025 Round 23 reference - this is the real read

**52-56 St Kilda. M.C.G. The 4-point game.** This is the freshest tape and the most-relevant tactical reference. Key features:

- Both sides scored fewer than 60 points - it was a contested, scrappy, low-scoring affair.
- St Kilda's win condition was their defensive structure - they held Richmond to 8.4 (52). Their own scoring (8.8 = 56) was modest but enough.
- The 4-point margin is the closest game between these two sides since the 2024 Round 5 Norwood Oval (-7).
- Richmond's lineup that day shares ~70% personnel with the listed 2026 squad. Sam Lalor, Seth Campbell, Sam Banks, Tyler Sonsie, Hugo Ralphsmith, Kane McAuliffe - the next-generation Richmond players were all there.
- The plan that *almost* worked: Richmond shortened the field, low entries, contested-mark-or-stoppage. The plan that *didn't* work: Sinclair (~32 disposals on the day per the 2025 averages) was not pressured enough at the wing.

The 2025 R23 game is the brief in miniature: if the Tigers can drag a Saints opponent into a low-scoring contest, the game stays winnable. The 2025 R4 result (-82) is what happens if it gets opened up.

## Tactical implications of the H2H

1. **The 2025 R23 game is the script.** A low-scoring contested contest. Richmond can survive at low scoreboard; cannot survive in shootouts.
2. **Docklands historically has favoured the Saints in the 2020s.** Strip the 2018-2021 dynasty era and Richmond have not won here against St Kilda in the modern phase.
3. **Saints win when Sinclair is unpressured at the wing.** Every blowout in the recent ledger (2024 R23, 2025 R4) features the Saints' midfield free-running. The R23 2025 4-point game features pressure on Sinclair and a low Saints score.
4. **2020s era is structurally R2-S7.** This is the most-relevant sample and the data says Saints are clearly the better team in this stretch. The brief is to **steal one game from the better team**.

## Caveats

- **Round numbers in finals**: finals games carry text labels (e.g. "Semi Final") in the source data. They are included in the H2H count and labelled accordingly.
- **Venue strings**: "Docklands" covers Marvel Stadium, Etihad Stadium, Telstra Dome, Colonial Stadium under their various commercial names since 2000. All resolve to the same physical venue.
- **No advanced stats for early matches**: pre-2017 matches in this dataset are score-and-quarter-by-quarter only at the team level. Tactical reads about contested ball, marks, etc. for the 2025 R23 game are inferred from the player-level box scores aggregated from `data/player_data/*_performance_details.csv`.
- The all-time picture (1908+) is dominated by pre-1980 Richmond dominance and is of limited tactical relevance. The 2021-2026 nine-game sample is the only directly comparable era.
